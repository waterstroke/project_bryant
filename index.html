A page about Tory Bryant

My art encompasses many forms of media, including including hand drawing, painting, graphic design, photography, pottery, sculpture, film and fabrication. I endlessly experiment with traditional and unusual techniques. I love to combine pastels and oils in a single work. I sometimes draw out my image with charcoal, blocking it out in oil paint, then I apply layers of pastel. glazes and thick bits of paint. I find a variety of ways to successfully obtain a richness in color, translucency and surface effects.
