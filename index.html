A page about TORY BRYANT

The Still life

Still life is where my life in art began. My color, my discovery, my success was all tied up in my first really successful painting. From that day I found my subject of old nostalgic toys, suckers and translucent bottles to keep me going for a very long time. That was my gateway.

The cityscape

I use photography in my cityscapes to look at my objects subjectively. I uses the idea of the accidental cropping of a subject in a snapshot, to inform and influence my final paintings. This cropping tends to give a more natural or authentic arrangement of her objects - much more like real life, and therefore more dynamic and unpredictable. While on site, I spend time sketching out the scene. Making notes of color relations and make myself a map of what I need to inform my work.
