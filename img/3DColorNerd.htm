Tory Bryant

3D Color development texture lead

 

Based out of Portland, Tory Bryant, consistently creates color prints that "pushes the technical boundaries of what 3D printing technology was made for."

Tory Jean Bryant is an award-winning artist, which includes an Oscar for her department that she plays an integral hand.  Tory’s current role is 3D Development Color Lead at Laika Studios.

"Brian Mclean has credited the film’s lead texture artist, Tory Bryant, for figuring out how to recreate the subtleties of the hand-painted maquettes through the 3D printing process, something that didn’t seem possible just a few years ago.

-Cartoon Brew
